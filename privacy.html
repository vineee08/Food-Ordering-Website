Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Sardaarji's Kitchen.

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our services (like visiting our website, using our app, or placing an order), we may collect certain personal information. This typically includes:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Order Information: Details of the items you purchase, order time, payment method (we do not store full credit card numbers), and any special instructions.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing activity on our site (collected through cookies and similar technologies).
  • Account Information: If you create an account, your username, password (hashed), and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for several purposes:

  • To process and deliver your food orders accurately and efficiently.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, account, or inquiries.
  • To improve our website, app, menu, and overall service based on user behavior and feedback.
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., remembering your address or past orders).
  • To send promotional offers or updates, but only if you have opted-in to receive them.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies (small text files stored on your device) and similar technologies. These help us:

  • Remember your login status and cart contents.
  • Understand how users interact with our site to make improvements.
  • Analyze the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

You have control over cookies. Most web browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies through their settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website (e.g., keeping items in your cart).

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We value your trust and do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With companies that help us operate our business, such as delivery partners (sharing address and contact details for delivery) and payment processors (handling payment information securely). These providers are obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process (like a subpoena or court order).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information might be transferred as part of the transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. This includes using encryption for sensitive data transmission (like payments) and restricting access to your information to authorized personnel only. However, please be aware that no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your information. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to contact us using the details provided on our contact page.

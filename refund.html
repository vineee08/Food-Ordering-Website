Refund Policy

Information regarding order cancellations and refunds at Sardaarji's Kitchen.

Effective Date: April 23, 2025

1. Order Cancellations

We understand that plans can change. You may cancel your order through our website or mobile app within 5 minutes of placing it for a full refund. After this 5-minute window, your order preparation typically begins, and cancellations may no longer be possible.

To cancel, please go to your order history or contact our customer support immediately at +91 1234567890.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are primarily issued in the following situations:

  • Order Cancellation: If you successfully cancel your order within the 5-minute timeframe mentioned above.
  • Incorrect Items Received: If you receive items different from what you ordered.
  • Damaged or Spoiled Items: If the food items arrive in a damaged or clearly spoiled condition, rendering them unfit for consumption.
  • Missing Items: If items paid for are missing from your delivery.
  • Failed Delivery: In rare cases where delivery could not be completed due to issues solely on our end or our delivery partner's end (excluding cases where the customer provided incorrect details or was unavailable).

Please note that dissatisfaction with taste or personal preference does not typically qualify for a refund, although we value your feedback immensely.

3. Reporting Issues & Requesting Refunds

  • For issues related to incorrect, damaged, spoiled, or missing items, please contact our customer support within 30 minutes of receiving your order.
  • We may require photographic evidence of the issue (e.g., pictures of damaged packaging or incorrect items) to process your request efficiently.
  • Please provide your Order ID and a clear description of the problem when contacting support.

4. Refund Process

  • Once your refund request is approved, the amount will typically be processed back to your original payment method (Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Wallet, etc.).
  • Refund processing times can vary depending on your bank or payment provider, but generally take 3-7 business days to reflect in your account.
  • For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds may be processed via bank transfer or store credit, based on the discussion with customer support.

5. Contact Information

For all cancellation and refund requests, or if you have any questions regarding this policy, please reach out to our customer support team:

  • Phone: +91 1234567890 (11:00 AM - 10:00 PM)
  • Email: support@sardaarjikitchen.com (or info@sardaarjikitchen.com)
  • Contact Form: Via our Contact Us page.
